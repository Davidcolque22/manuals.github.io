MANUAL FOR THE CORRECT USE OF PERFUME

HOW TO USE

Apply generously every day

PRECAUTIONS

Keep out of reach of negative people

BENEFITS

Improves You confidence and your mood

CONSEJOS

FIRST

Store your perfume in a cool place, away from humidity and radiation.

SECOND

Place the perfume container inside your case.

THIRD

Apply the fragance after getting out of the shower, on your dry skin for greater effect.

ROOM

To make the scent permate your skin more, use unscented body cream and then apply the perfume.

FIFTH

Apply to strategic areas, behind the ears, neck and wrists, as these are pulse points, which help prolong the duration of the fragrance.
